palak-rice

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup cooked rice
  2. 1 cup chopped/steamed palak
  3. 2 tbsp oil
  4. 1 tsp fennel
  5. 1 onion (finely chopped)
  6. 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  7. spices
  8. 4 tomato (finely chopped)

How to Prepare?
Step 1 Cook the rice. Steam the palak. Step 2 Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Saute onion, tomato and garlic in olive oil until lightly browned, 5 to 7 minutes. Step 3 Add the cooked rice to the pan. Mix well and wait for 2-3 minutes. Serve it hot. : )
