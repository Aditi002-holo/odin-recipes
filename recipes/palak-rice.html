palak-rice

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup chopped/steamed palak
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp fennel
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • spices
  • 4 tomato (finely chopped)

How to Prepare?

Step 1 Cook the rice. Steam the palak.
Step 2 Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Saute onion, tomato and garlic in olive oil until lightly browned, 5 to 7 minutes.
Step 3 Add the cooked rice to the pan. Mix well and wait for 2-3 minutes. Serve it hot. : )
